Who We Are

The Virginia Data Protection Agency is dedicated to protecting your consumer data rights
under Virginia's Data Consumer Protection Act.

We oversee and enforce the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (CDPA) to ensure transparency and accountability. Our mission is to empower consumers with control over their personal data.

About the Virginia CDPA

The Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act grants consumers the rights to access, correct, delete, and opt-out of personal data collection practices.
